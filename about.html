The Story of Martier's Name

The 18th Century woman was the most influential and well-respected member of her sex in the history of civilization. Women discreetly advanced their status and influence on society, becoming authors, artists, doctors and entrepreneurs.

It was at this time, in the center of Paris, that an elegant boutique named MARTIER was born, featuring a beautiful selection of women's clothing by designers from every corner of Europe. Luxurious fasions were much in demand. Technological advanced such as Jacquard look enabled the production of larger qualities and more varied styles of patterned fabrics. Others items, such as women's shoes, become design statements rather than merely functional attire, and were decorated with fine fabrics and embroodery. The name MARTIER become sysnonymous with style and sensuality.
